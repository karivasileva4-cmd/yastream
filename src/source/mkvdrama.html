Synopsis

At Seorin High School, five close friends stumble upon a mysterious app called Girigo, rumored to grant any wish. Tempted by its promise, they soon discover a terrifying truth—anyone who uses it is doomed to face sudden and unavoidable death.

As fear and doubt begin to fracture their bond, the group races to uncover the origins and rules behind the app. With each choice carrying deadly consequences, they are forced to confront their deepest desires and the price they’re willing to pay. Escaping the curse may require sacrifices far greater than they ever imagined.
